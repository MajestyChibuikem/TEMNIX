Project Overview

We designed and deployed an end-to-end AI customer support system for a growing e-commerce company drowning in support tickets. The solution combines a natural language processing chatbot with intelligent ticket classification and routing. Built with Python, LangChain, and a React dashboard, the system handles order tracking, returns, FAQs, and complex issue escalation — learning and improving from every interaction.

Scope of Work

Temnix architected the entire AI support pipeline — from the conversational AI engine to the analytics dashboard that gives managers real-time visibility into support operations.

Key deliverables included:

  • NLP Chatbot Engine: A LangChain-powered conversational AI trained on the company's knowledge base, capable of handling multi-turn conversations, understanding intent, and resolving 70% of customer inquiries autonomously.

  • Intelligent Ticket Routing: ML-based classification system that categorizes incoming tickets by urgency, topic, and complexity — routing them to the right specialist team in under 2 seconds.

  • Real-Time Analytics Dashboard: A management dashboard showing chatbot performance, resolution rates, customer satisfaction scores, and escalation patterns with actionable insights.

Results & Impact

The AI Customer Support system reduced average response time from 4 hours to under 45 seconds. The chatbot handles 70% of all incoming inquiries without human intervention, freeing up the support team to focus on complex cases. Customer satisfaction scores improved by 34%, and the company saved an estimated $180,000 annually in support costs. The system processes over 2,000 conversations daily and continues to improve through reinforcement learning from customer feedback.

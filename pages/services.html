FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Find quick answers here. Common questions are explained clearly with helpful details. Explore guidance, tips, and support anytime.

What AI services do you offer?

We build AI chatbots, automate document processing, develop predictive analytics models, and integrate LLMs into existing business workflows. Every solution is custom-built for your specific use case and data.

How long does an AI project take?

Simple chatbot integrations can be deployed in 2–4 weeks. Custom ML models and full automation pipelines typically take 6–12 weeks, depending on data readiness and complexity.

Do I need a lot of data to get started?

Not always. Many of our solutions leverage pre-trained LLMs and can work with your existing documents, emails, and databases. During our AI audit, we assess your data and recommend the best approach.

Will AI replace my team?

No — our goal is augmentation, not replacement. AI handles repetitive, time-consuming tasks so your team can focus on high-value strategic work. Most clients see their teams become more productive, not smaller.

What makes Temnix different from other AI agencies?

We focus on measurable business outcomes, not just technology. Every project starts with ROI analysis, and we only build what delivers real value. Our AI-augmented team means faster delivery with lower overhead.
